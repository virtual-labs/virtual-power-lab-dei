Procedure
1. To make connections
Click on radio buttons "1 & R", "2 & Y" and "3 & B" respectively
2. Click on 3-Phase switch to close
3. Observe Synchronous Machine rotation (Clockwise)
4. Click on 3-Phase switch to open and Click on 2-Phase switch (DC Switch) at right hand side
Observe rotation (Anticlockwise)
5. To make same direction of rotation, change phase sequence
6. Open DC switch
7. Click on "clear Connections" Button
8. Click on radio buttons "1 & R", "2 & B" and "3 & Y" respectively
9. Close 3-phase switch
Now rotation is Anticlockwise
10. Hence phase sequence is same
