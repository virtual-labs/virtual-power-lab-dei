Virtual Power Lab Development:

Prof. Chaturvedi and his team developed a virtual lab in the area of electrical power systems under the initiative of MHRD, Govt. of India. The purpose of this lab is to help the students of engineering colleges where quality labs/staff not available or wants to do engineering through correspondence or in distance mode.
