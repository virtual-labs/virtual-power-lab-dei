Dayalbagh Educational Institute Soft computing Engineering Laboratory
(DEISEL)
  • Aim

    1. To exploit the tolerance for imprecision uncertainty, approximate reasoning and partial truth to achieve tractability, robustness,low solution cost, and close resemblance with human like decision making.

    2. To find an approximate solution to an imprecisely/precisely formulated problem.

  • Challenge

    1. The challenge is to exploit the tolerance for imprecision by devising methods of computation which lead to an acceptable solution at low cost. This, in essence, is the guiding principle of soft computing.
