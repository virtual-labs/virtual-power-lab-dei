The objectives of the book are:

  • To provide an essential update on the Future of Mankind
  • To identify the Strategic Theology with direction and dimension within the context of the fast Technological Changes
  • To take a lead in the on-going thinking process on how Theology can drive the Future of mankind
  • To highlight the significance of the contributions of Theology; link between Religion and Future of Mankind and the Teachings of Bhagwad Gita and Sant Mat; Agriculture Operation; Social Service through top–down, bottom–up and middle–out approach.
